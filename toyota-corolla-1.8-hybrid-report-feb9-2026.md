## 3. What to Check When Buying (Ekspertiz)

### ‚úÖ Must-Check Items

1. **Battery Health**
   - Check hybrid battery status at Toyota service
   - Ask for battery test report
   - Avoid cars with 100k+ km without battery check

2. **Service History**
   - Toyota authorized service records preferred
   - Regular hybrid system maintenance
   - CVT transmission service history

3. **Electronics**
   - Test all multimedia functions
   - Check touchscreen responsiveness
   - Verify climate control works properly

4. **Suspension**
   - Test on rough roads if possible
   - Listen for unusual noises
   - Check shock absorbers

5. **Body & Paint**
   - Check for accident repairs
   - Panel gaps consistency
   - Rust (especially undercarriage)

6. **Tires & Brakes**
   - Tire condition (expensive to replace all 4)
   - Brake pad thickness
   - Brake disc condition

### üö© Red Flags

- **No service history** ‚Äî walk away
- **Battery warnings** on dashboard
- **Transmission jerks** or delays
- **Accident repairs** without proper documentation
- **High km + old age** combination (150k+ km on 2018-2019 models)

7. **Not for enthusiasts** ‚Äî If you enjoy driving, this car will disappoint. It's an appliance, not a driver's car.

## 12. Buying Guide: How to Purchase Safely in Turkey

### Before Viewing the Car

**Research Phase:**
- Set a realistic budget (include insurance, notary, maintenance)
- Check market prices on arabam.com, sahibinden.com
- Identify 3-5 suitable options
- Check Tramer (damage history) via SMS to 5664 with chassis number

**Questions to Ask Seller:**
- Exact km and where driven (city/highway)
- Accident history ‚Äî which parts changed?
- Tramer damage amount
- Timing belt/clutch maintenance done?
- Spare key and winter tires included?

### During Inspection

**Visual Checks:**
| Area | What to Look For | Red Flag |
|------|------------------|----------|
| **Body** | Panel gaps, paint mismatch | Different shades = accident |
| **Roof/Pillars** | Any repairs | Repairs here = serious accident |
| **Tires** | Tread depth, DOT date | <1.6mm illegal, >5 years old |
| **Interior** | Steering/pedal wear | Doesn't match km = rolled back |
| **Engine Bay** | Leaks, corrosion | Oil/coolant leaks |
| **Exhaust** | Smoke color | Blue=oil, Black=fuel, White=coolant |

**Test Drive Checklist:**
- [ ] Radio off ‚Äî listen for noises
- [ ] Drive over bumps ‚Äî suspension sounds?
- [ ] Highway speed ‚Äî vibrations?
- [ ] Brakes ‚Äî soft pedal? pulling?
- [ ] Steering ‚Äî centered? responsive?
- [ ] Acceleration ‚Äî smooth? delayed?

### Common Scams to Avoid

| Scam | How It Works | Protection |
|------|--------------|------------|
| **Kapora Trap** | "Send deposit to hold car" | Never send money before seeing car |
| **Fake Expertise** | "My expert friend will check" | Choose your own expert |
| **KM Rollback** | Lowering odometer | Check service history, wear signs |
| **Plate Transfer** | Car has liens/loans | Check with chassis number, not plate |
| **Stolen Car** | Fake documents | Verify chassis number matches everywhere |

### Required Documents (2026)

**Must Have:**
- ‚úÖ Ruhsat (Vehicle Registration)
- ‚úÖ Valid ID (both buyer and seller)
- ‚úÖ Traffic Insurance (in buyer's name ‚Äî NEW 2024 RULE)
- ‚úÖ Valid Inspection (Muayene)
- ‚úÖ No Tax Fines (MTV/traffic)
- ‚úÖ No Liens/Rehin

**NEW RULE (Dec 2024):** You cannot complete notary sale without active traffic insurance in YOUR name. Get insurance before notary.

### Notary Process

1. **Safe Payment:** Use "Guvenli Odeme Sistemi" (Secure Payment System)
   - Money held in escrow
   - Released after notary confirms sale
2. **Document Check:** Notary verifies IDs, insurance, no liens
3. **Sign Contract:** Both parties sign
4. **Pay Fees:**
   - Notary fee: ~1.869 TL (fixed)
   - Nispi Harc: 0.2% of car price
5. **Get New Ruhsat:** Issued immediately in your name

### After Purchase Checklist

- [ ] Get new ruhsat from notary
- [ ] Arrange Kasko (full coverage insurance)
- [ ] Change plates (if desired)
- [ ] First maintenance (oil, filters, fluids)
- [ ] Register for EDS (electronic toll system)
